Technology

Description

IvyCPG is a mobile application designed to streamline sales, distribution, and order management in the FMCG sector. It automates processes like sales reporting, inventory management, and order fulfillment. The app is optimized for high performance, supporting offline capabilities, and ensuring efficient pricing, schemes, discounts, and tax calculations. Deployed in India, Indonesia, and Dubai, it serves prominent clients like PNG, Bimbo, Mars, and Reckitt.
